About us

Integrated delivery across construction, marine works, and real estate

Mahfas Investment Limited is a real estate development and construction company headquartered in Lagos, with branches across Nigeria. We deliver residential, commercial, marine, and infrastructure projects with disciplined teams, dependable equipment, and modern systems.

Alongside our construction and development services, our capabilities span land reclamation, channel clearing, shoreline protection, hydrographic support, sand stockpiling, and site development for private, corporate, and government clients. Every engagement is shaped around quality standards, cost effectiveness, and clear communication from planning through handover.

  • Lagos head office and Lekki project presence
  • Abuja legislative quarters and site operations
  • Enugu regional office and delivery footprint
