About us

Established expertise across Nigeria

Mahfas Investment Limited is a real estate development and construction company headquartered in Lagos, with branches across Nigeria. We handle real estate and construction projects to meet client expectations and satisfaction within agreed timelines—supported by our computerized systems and highly trained workforce.

Our experienced, disciplined management team is capable of delivering every target to make each project a success. From residential estates to commercial builds, we combine technical rigor with clear communication at every stage.

  • Lagos head office & Lekki presence
  • Abuja area & site offices
  • Enugu regional office
